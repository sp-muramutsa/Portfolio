Three-tier Voting Simulation: Plurality — Runoff — Tideman
In Plurality, the "winner-take-all" i.e. the most voted candidate wins.

In Runoff, the least favorite candidates are eliminated until the winner emerges.

In Tideman, the winner is whoever would've won the most head-to-head matchups against every other candidate.
